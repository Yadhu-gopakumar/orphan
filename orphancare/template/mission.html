Our Mission

At Project Orphan Care Network, we’re committed to making sure every child who’s lost their family feels loved and supported. Our goal is to provide not just the basics like food and shelter but also to nurture their dreams and help them grow into confident, capable adults. We work hand-in-hand with communities and partners to offer education, emotional support, and a sense of belonging. Our mission is to turn every child’s potential into reality and help them build a hopeful future.
